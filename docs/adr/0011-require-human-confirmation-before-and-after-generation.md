# Require human confirmation before and after generation

The Web Studio will require a user to confirm an immutable generation brief
before a worker schedules rendering, including output count, camera angles,
lighting intensity, and building-use distribution. A separately validated
package must then receive publication confirmation after its quality summary and
contact sheet are reviewed.

## Considered Options

- Automatically launching and publishing a valid job would be faster but does
  not give the user control over the distribution they intend to train on.
